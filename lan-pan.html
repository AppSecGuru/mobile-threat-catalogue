--- layout: threat-list title: LANs and PANs ---

WiFi: WiFi is a wireless local area networking (WLAN) technology based on the IEEE 802.11 series of standards.

Bluetooth: Bluetooth is a medium-range, lower power, wireless communication technology.

FC: NFC is a short range wireless communication technology that is commonly used for mobile wallet technologies and peripheral configuration, although a number of other applications exist.
